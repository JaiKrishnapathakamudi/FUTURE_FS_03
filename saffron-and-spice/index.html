Our Story

A Legacy of Taste
& Tradition

Since 2010, Saffron & Spice has been synonymous with extraordinary dining experiences in Hyderabad. What began as a dream to preserve India's culinary heritage has grown into the city's most celebrated dining destination.

"Every dish we create is a love letter to the land — its spices, its soil, its soul. We cook not just to fill plates, but to create memories that last a lifetime."

— Chef Arjun Mehta, Executive Chef & Founder

FAQ

Frequently Asked

Do I need to make a reservation in advance?+
Walk-ins are welcome on weekdays. However, we strongly recommend reserving your table at least 48 hours in advance, especially for weekends, special occasions, or private dining rooms, to avoid disappointment.
Do you offer vegetarian and vegan options?+
Absolutely. Over 40% of our menu is vegetarian, and we have dedicated vegan, Jain-friendly and gluten-free options. Our chefs are happy to customise dishes on request. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when booking.
Is parking available?+
Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all dining guests. Self-parking is also available in our private basement lot with capacity for 80+ vehicles.
Can I host a private event or corporate dinner?+
We have two private dining rooms — The Mughal Suite (up to 20 guests) and The Garden Pavilion (up to 50 guests) — perfect for corporate events, weddings, and celebrations. Contact our events team for custom packages.
What is your cancellation policy?+
We ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Private dining room bookings require 48 hours notice. We understand plans change — please call or WhatsApp us and we'll do our best to accommodate.
