Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 November 2024  ·  Effective from: 1 November 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Saffron & Spice Fine Dining ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores and protects personal information provided through our website at saffronandspice.com and through table reservation enquiries.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Make a table reservation (name, phone number, email, date, guest count, special requests)
  • Submit a contact form (name, email, phone, message)
  • Subscribe to our newsletter (email address)
  • Contact us via WhatsApp or telephone

We may also automatically collect non-personal data such as browser type, device type, pages visited, and time on site through standard web analytics (e.g. Google Analytics, if enabled).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Confirm, manage and fulfil your table reservations
  • Respond to your enquiries and messages
  • Send reservation confirmations and reminders via phone or WhatsApp
  • Send our newsletter (only if you explicitly subscribed)
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Reservation and contact form data submitted through this website is currently stored in our internal reservation management system. We retain reservation records for a maximum of 2 years for operational purposes, after which they are securely deleted.

Newsletter subscriber emails are retained until you unsubscribe. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us directly.

4. Cookies

Our website uses only functional cookies — specifically, a small localStorage entry to remember your dark/light mode preference. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies without your consent.

Google Fonts and Unsplash (image CDN) are loaded from their respective servers and are subject to their own privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is transmitted to these services.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps (Embed) — for showing our restaurant location. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. Fonts are loaded from Google's servers.
  • WhatsApp — clicking the WhatsApp button opens your WhatsApp app. Messages are subject to WhatsApp/Meta's privacy policy.
  • Unsplash — stock images served from Unsplash CDN.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information, including HTTPS encryption on all pages, secure server configurations, and access controls. While we take reasonable precautions, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: privacy@saffronandspice.com

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
