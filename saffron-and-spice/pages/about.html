Our Story

Who We Are

Rooted in Tradition,
Driven by Passion

Saffron & Spice was born from a simple belief: that food is the most honest expression of culture. For fourteen years, we have honoured that belief every single day.

Our Mission
To Nourish & Delight

To serve extraordinary food in a space that feels like home — warm, welcoming and consistently exceptional. We aim to make every guest feel celebrated, regardless of the occasion. From a quiet lunch for one to a grand family feast, the quality of experience never wavers.

Our Vision
India's Living Table

To become the definitive home of authentic Indian cuisine for a new generation — a place where culinary heritage is preserved with rigour and reimagined with creativity. We envision a restaurant that future chefs and food lovers will look back on as a landmark institution.

Executive Chef

Arjun Mehta

Founder & Executive Chef

Chef Arjun's journey began in his grandmother's kitchen in Lucknow, where he learned that great food starts with patience and love. After culinary school in Mumbai, he trained under Michelin-starred chef Marcus Wareing in London before returning to India with a vision.

He founded Saffron & Spice in 2010 with a team of three and a menu of twelve dishes. Today, that team numbers fifty and the menu spans six categories — but the philosophy remains unchanged: every plate must tell a story.

Chef Arjun was named India's Best Regional Chef by Culinary India Awards in 2019 and 2022, and appears regularly on food television and culinary panels across Asia.

Our Journey

From Dream to Legacy

2010
The First Flame

Chef Arjun opens Saffron & Spice with a 40-cover dining room, three kitchen staff, and twelve dishes. The queue on opening night stretches down Banjara Hills Road.

2013
Expansion & Recognition

Capacity doubles to 90 covers. Wins first Times Food Award. The Dum Biryani Royale is featured in The Hindu as "the definitive Hyderabadi biryani."

2016
Private Dining & Events

The Mughal Suite private dining room opens. Saffron & Spice begins hosting curated chef's table experiences and wine pairing dinners.

2019
National Acclaim

Chef Arjun wins India's Best Regional Chef. Restaurant named in Condé Nast's Top 50. The Garden Pavilion opens for outdoor fine dining.

2022
Farm-to-Table Commitment

Launches direct partnerships with 12 certified organic farms across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh. 90% of produce now sourced within 200km.

2024
14 Years & Counting

Over 24,000 guests served annually. 8 national awards. A new seasonal tasting menu launches, pushing the boundaries of modern Indian cuisine.
