Getting Here
1
From Hyderabad Airport (RGIA): Take NH 44 towards city. Exit at Mehdipatnam, head north on Banjara Hills Road. Total ~45 minutes.
2
From Secunderabad Railway Station: Take the metro (Blue Line) to Ameerpet, then auto/cab to Banjara Hills Road No. 12. Total ~30 minutes.
3
From HITEC City: Take Road No. 36 south to Jubilee Hills, then follow Banjara Hills Road. Total ~20 minutes by car.
4
Parking: Valet available at entrance. Basement self-parking for 80+ vehicles. No parking charges for diners.
